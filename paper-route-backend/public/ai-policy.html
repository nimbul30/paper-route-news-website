The Paper Route News

AI Policy Statement

At The Paper Route News, we are committed to leveraging technology to provide clear, balanced, and efficient news. This policy outlines how we use Artificial Intelligence (AI) in our products, such as the News Synthesizer, to enhance your understanding of the world.

1. AI-Powered Summarization

Our core AI feature is the topic synthesizer, which aggregates articles from diverse sources and generates a single, concise summary. The goal is to give you a comprehensive overview of a news story quickly. These summaries are generated by Large Language Models (LLMs) and are designed to be neutral and factual.

2. Human Oversight

While AI generates the initial summaries, we believe in the irreplaceability of human judgment. All AI-generated content is subject to review, editing, and fact-checking by our team of human editors before publication. No article is published without human oversight.

3. Bias and Perspective

We recognize that AI models can reflect biases from their training data. To counter this, we provide features like the "Perspective Spectrum" to show the political leaning of the sources used in a synthesis. Our goal is to make media bias transparent, helping you understand how different sides are reporting on an issue.

4. Transparency

We are committed to being transparent about our use of AI. Whenever content is significantly aided by AI, it will be clearly disclosed. We will always provide links to the original source articles used for synthesis.

5. Policy Updates

The field of AI is constantly evolving. We will regularly review and update this policy to reflect our current practices and the latest developments in technology.
